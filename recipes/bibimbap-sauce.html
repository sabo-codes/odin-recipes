My Korean Kitchen's Bibimbap Sauce

Description

Simple recipe for a mildly spicy red sauce to use with your bibimbap and noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup toasted sesame seeds
  • 4 tsp vinegar, apple cider vinegar or rice vinegar
  • 4 tsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix them well.
  2. Enjoy!
